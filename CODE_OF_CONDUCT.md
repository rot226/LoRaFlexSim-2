# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

To foster an open, safe, and welcoming community, we commit to ensuring that participation in LoRaFlexSim remains respectful, constructive, and inclusive for all contributors and community members.

## Expected Conduct

Community members are expected to:

- demonstrate respectful conduct and professional courtesy;
- engage in technical discussion in good faith and accept constructive feedback;
- communicate clearly, calmly, and productively throughout the review process;
- contribute to documentation and discussions in a way that supports newcomers and the broader community.

## Unacceptable Conduct

The following behaviors are not acceptable, including but not limited to:

- harassment, intimidation, insults, or personal attacks;
- discriminatory, degrading, or demeaning language or behavior;
- disclosure of private or confidential information without explicit authorization;
- any disruptive conduct that creates a hostile, unsafe, or unproductive environment.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other community spaces associated with this repository.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for enforcement of this Code of Conduct. They may moderate, edit, or remove contributions that do not comply with these standards, and may take any reasonable enforcement action necessary to protect the community.

## Reporting

To report problematic behavior, use an appropriate private reporting channel provided by the maintainers. If a `SECURITY.md` document or contact guidance exists, follow it to identify the correct reporting channel.
